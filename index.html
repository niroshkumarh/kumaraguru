KUMARAGURU INSTITUTION's

Kumaraguru Institutions, a multifaceted hub of learning instituted in 1984 with Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), KCT Business School (KCT BS) in 2005, Kumaraguru Institute of Agriculture (KIA) in 2014 Kumaraguru College of Liberal Arts and Science (KCLAS) in 2018 and Kumaraguru School of Business (KSB) in 2022, is an integral part of Sakthi Group established by visionary statesman Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam.

Commemorating 41 years of educational excellence, Kumaraguru Institutions remains committed to its founding vision of empowering conscientious young minds through transformative learning experience.

OUR Ecosystem

Situated across three sprawling campuses spanning 280 acres, Kumaraguru Institutions offers 25 Undergraduate programmes, 20 Postgraduate programmes and as many research programmes. Presently, more than 8000 students are imparted quality education in the domains of engineering, technology, management, science, agriculture, innovation, entrepreneurship, liberal arts and humanities. The 35,000+ Kumaraguru alumni spread across 47 countries have forged a global footprint, embodying the institution’s ethos and values.

RIDE at Kumaraguru:

A Thriving Ecosystem of Possibilities At Kumaraguru Institutions, RIDE is not just a concept but a lived reality, seamlessly woven into the institution’s ethos. The ecosystem at Kumaraguru thrives on the synergy of these four pillars, creating an environment where students, faculty, and industry partners converge to explore, create, and transform thereby becoming creative thinkers, problem-solvers, and changemakers.

Partner with Kumaraguru Institutions: Shaping the Future of Global Education



At Kumaraguru Institutions (KI), we believe in the power of collaboration to create transformative learning experiences. With a legacy of academic excellence, innovation, and global engagement, we are committed to building meaningful partnerships that drive impact in education, research, and student development. We invite universities, research institutions, industries, and organizations worldwide to join us in shaping the future of higher education through strategic collaborations that foster global learning, innovation, and knowledge exchange.

With state-of-the-art research centres, innovation hubs, industry partnerships, and incubation cells, Kumaraguru empowers its students and faculty to explore new frontiers and bring impactful ideas to life.
