KUMARAGURU INSTITUTION's

Kumaraguru Institutions, a multifaceted hub of learning instituted in 1984 with Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), KCT Business School (KCT BS) in 2005, Kumaraguru Institute of Agriculture (KIA) in 2014 Kumaraguru College of Liberal Arts and Science (KCLAS) in 2018 and Kumaraguru School of Business (KSB) in 2022, is an integral part of Sakthi Group established by visionary statesman Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam.

Situated across three sprawling campuses spanning 280 acres, Kumaraguru Institutions offers 25 Undergraduate programmes, 20 Postgraduate programmes and as many research programmes. Presently, more than 8000 students are imparted quality education in the domains of engineering, technology, management, science, agriculture, innovation, entrepreneurship, liberal arts and humanities. The 35,000+ Kumaraguru alumni spread across 47 countries have forged a global footprint, embodying the institutionâ€™s ethos and values.

Commemorating 41 years of educational excellence, Kumaraguru Institutions remains committed to its founding vision of empowering conscientious young minds through transformative learning experience.
